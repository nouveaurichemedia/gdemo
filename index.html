ABOUT

Global Tecknopress Limited (GTL) is a leading printing press located in Ikorodu, Lagos Nigeria.

The company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2011, is established on a
foundation of excellence, to provide pleasant customer experience, quality products and services, under best work practices and procedures.
GTL have maintained injury free records for the seven years of operations, following GTL’s profound commitment
to safety of personnel and equipment, and remains committed to maintaining the record.
GTL is recognized as one of the most managed presses in the industry for providing
conducive work environment for both staff and clients.

We pride ourselves as an organization with pre-press, press and post-press facilities
that we believe would not only meet, but exceed our client’s expectations in quality and delivery.
